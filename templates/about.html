About Us

Welcome to doodle wear, where every stitch tells a story and every design is a masterpiece crafted by hand. Nestled in the heart of Switzerland, our brand is a celebration of artisanal excellence, sustainability, and a commitment to weaving narratives into every garment.

At the heart of our creative journey is our team of passionate artisans, led by our visionary CEO, who meticulously draw on each shirt, transforming it into a canvas of unique expression. Our dedication to the art of hand-drawn designs sets us apart, ensuring that every piece is a wearable work of art, with no two shirts being exactly alike.

Embracing the essence of Swiss craftsmanship, we proudly declare that all our creations are Swiss-made. This commitment to quality and precision echoes throughout our entire process, from sourcing eco-friendly materials to the careful creation of limited-edition shirts that resonate with individuality and purpose.

Speaking of eco-friendliness, sustainability is not just a buzzword for us—it's a way of life. We prioritize environmentally conscious practices, striving to minimize our ecological footprint and contribute to a greener future. From our choice of materials to our packaging, every decision reflects our commitment to a more sustainable and responsible fashion industry.

What makes our shirts truly exceptional is the story they carry. Each limited-edition release unfolds a chapter in our narrative, connecting wearers to a broader tale of creativity, inspiration, and the journey of our brand. It's more than just a shirt; it's a wearable adventure, a piece of wearable art that invites you to be part of our ongoing story.

Join us at doodle wear as we redefine fashion, one hand-drawn design at a time. Wear a story, wear a statement, wear the essence of Swiss craftsmanship and eco-conscious elegance.
